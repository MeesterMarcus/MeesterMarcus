## 📱 Apps I've Built

### 🏋️ <a href="https://ironstack.app">IronStack</a>

A minimalist, offline-first strength training log built from real lifting experience.

* Fast, efficient set logging
* Warm-up sets, rest timers, and notes
* Automatic PR tracking and history
* AI workout generation and training insights
* Privacy-first, your data stays on your device with export and import support
* Apple Watch integration

Built for lifters who want something simple, fast, and focused.

### 🌙 <a href="https://restwisesleep.com/">RestWise</a>

A structured sleep program designed to reduce sleep anxiety and break the insomnia cycle.

* CBT-I and ACT-inspired approach
* 30-day guided program
* Daily reflections and check-ins
* Focused on building confidence around sleep

Built to help people stop fighting sleep and regain control.

---

### 🧠 <a href="https://gradualexposurehelp.com/">Arc</a>

A structured, private tool for practicing gradual exposure and building tolerance over time.

* Build and manage your own exposure ladder
* Log attempts with predicted, peak, and end anxiety
* Track outcomes, notes, and progress over time
* Process-first approach focused on consistency and repetition
* Lightweight motivation without gamifying recovery
* Export and import your data (JSON)
* Privacy-first, data stored locally on your device

Built to help users follow a clear plan, stay consistent, and make real progress through exposure practice.
